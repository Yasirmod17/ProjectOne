Club soccer was founded in 2011 by now Alums of the college Eli and Griffith. Sensing the need for a bridge between the intensity of varsity soccer and relaxed nature of intramurals, club soccer offers both without any written contract binding you to training sessions, team workouts... We hope you join. It is definately one of the more fun things on campus.
